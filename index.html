Welcome to: RiverGuide

A Community Driven Guidebook

Mission:

The goal of RiverGuide is to become the "go to" resource for the white water community. By pairing realtime flow data, updated hazard reports, and river descriptions provided by the community we hope to become the ideal resource for paddlers looking to find new runs both locally and while exploring new areas.
